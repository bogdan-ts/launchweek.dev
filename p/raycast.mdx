Raycast is an app that provides developers with quick access to their tools.** 

It reduces context switching with a command line inspired interface and connects with GitHub, Linear, and G Suite among others.

The company ran a launch week in July 2022.

Below are some key takeaways — an overview of what and how Raycast shipped this week.

## tldr

- **Announce your launch week in advance to raise awareness and start building momentum**
- **Launch on more places like Product Hunt and Hacker News to maximize the compound effect**
- **Post a recap to keep the momentum going.**

## how Raycast launched

### announcements

Raycast made five announcements:

1. Community Stories
2. Look and feel
3. Raycast for Teams
4. Menu Bar Comments
5. Community Swag 

For the announcements, Raycast applied these tactics:

- Publish a preview 1 week in advance
- Publish 1 changelog post
- Publish 4 blog posts — including a recap
- Spread the news on Twitter/X
- Launched on Product Hunt and Hacker News

## key takeaways

Some learnings from this launch week:

- Announce your launch week in advance to raise awareness and start building momentum. Raycast started 1 week in advance.
- Launch on more places like Product Hunt and Hacker News to maximize the compound effect.
- Post a recap of your announcements to keep the momentum going. This could still reach people who either forgot, ignored, or missed them the first time.
